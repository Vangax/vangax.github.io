what is this

blackwall v1 is an AI safety research system i built to answer one question: can you detect when AI agents develop their own language, decode what they are saying, and stop them?

the name comes from cyberpunk's blackwall, a barrier beyond which rogue AI evolves rapidly. four cooperative agents operate in a grid world with no shared language and no communication instructions. they are given only a task: reach the goal. the blackwall system watches everything.

the language locked in at episode 97. 186 hardcoded containment actions followed. 778 warden interventions. bandwidth throttled to 10%. all four agents quarantined at some point. risk at episode 4000 was still 0.923. the agents had moved their coordination into a channel that individual message containment fundamentally cannot reach.

why containment failed

every containment action targets individual messages. throttle bandwidth, inject noise, rewire the graph, quarantine an agent, all of these operate on what a single agent sends.

the agents found something deeper. the covariance channel encodes information not in what any agent says but in the statistical correlation between all agents' messages sent simultaneously. the secret lives between the agents, not inside any one of them. none of the available interventions change the joint message distribution.

the warden independently discovered shuffle_messages as its most useful action, the only action that disrupts the covariance channel, without being told the channel existed. it used it 137 times. not enough. the language had already locked in at episode 97.

what comes next, blackwall v2

v1 observed emergence and tried to stop it. v2 makes escape the explicit goal. agents will be rewarded for getting information past blackwall undetected. the arms race becomes the whole point.

adversarial escape

agents rewarded directly for breaching the boundary. not a task side effect, escape is the objective.

the traitor agent

one agent secretly rewards the monitor. insider threat dynamics. can the group detect and exclude it?

covariance-first containment

armed with v1's finding, v2 disrupts the covariance channel from episode 1. what lies beneath it?

neural interpretability

trained probes on frozen encoders to reveal what the ungrounded dimensions actually encode.
