neuroReactor: a real-time neural state interface

Developed a real-time EEG-based neural state decoding and visualization system using the BCI Competition IV 2a motor imagery dataset. Implemented a full signal processing pipeline including band-pass filtering, FBCSP feature extraction, and supervised deep embedding learning for motor imagery classification. Designed a continuous neural state modeling framework that streams decoded mental states in real-time, deriving additional metrics such as prediction entropy, volatility, confidence stability, and transition dynamics.

research/project

a bayesian Counterfactual Early-Warning System for ICU Deterioration

Developed a Bayesian early-warning system for ICU deterioration using irregular clinical time series from the MIMIC-IV dataset. The model integrates GRU-D to explicitly handle informative missingness and Monte Carlo dropout to quantify predictive uncertainty in time-dependent risk estimates. Beyond conventional prediction, the framework performs counterfactual intervention simulations to assess how early clinical actions could alter future ICU risk trajectories. Experimental results demonstrate dynamic risk escalation detection, calibrated uncertainty estimates, and clinically interpretable counterfactual risk reductions.
