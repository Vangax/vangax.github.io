ai safety · research project

blackwall v1: emergent communication detection & containment

built a five-phase system to detect, decode, and contain emergent language in multi-agent RL. four agents spontaneously developed a structured language — with compositionality, semantic grounding, and covert steganographic channels — that survived 186 containment actions and a learned adversary across 4,000 episodes. peak risk: 0.940. the language moved below the observable layer.

research/project

a bayesian Counterfactual Early-Warning System for ICU Deterioration

Developed a Bayesian early-warning system for ICU deterioration using irregular clinical time series from the MIMIC-IV dataset. The model integrates GRU-D to explicitly handle informative missingness and Monte Carlo dropout to quantify predictive uncertainty in time-dependent risk estimates.
